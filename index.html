How I got into Web Development

When I graduated from George Mason University I wasn't sure if the career I stuided for was the one I wanted to pursue. I graduated with a degree in Marketing and knew I enjoyed two things: design and product development. At Mason, one of my favorite classes was Internet Marketing. A part of the class was creating a landing page for a non-profit. The goal was to collect emails. Although the project was small, it opened a new avenue for me. Becoming a web developer never crossed my mind before that class and now having learned various languages it has only strengthened my passion for design and product development.
